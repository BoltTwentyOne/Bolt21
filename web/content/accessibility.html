Policies

Accessibility Statement

Last updated: December 2024

Our Commitment

Bolt21 is committed to accessibility and ensuring that all users, including those with disabilities, have an equal opportunity to use our wallet and access all information on this website.

Standards We Follow

We strive to meet Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. We continuously research and implement improvements across our app and website.

Accessibility Features

Our website and app include the following accessibility measures:

  • Alternative text - Images include descriptive alt text for screen readers
  • Color contrast - We maintain a minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio for text
  • Descriptive links - Link text describes the destination or action
  • Consistent navigation - Pages follow a predictable layout pattern
  • Keyboard navigation - All features are accessible via keyboard
  • Focus indicators - Visible focus states for interactive elements
  • Skip links - Skip to main content links for keyboard users
  • Responsive design - Content adapts to different screen sizes and zoom levels
  • No auto-play - No content auto-plays or moves unexpectedly

Mobile App Accessibility

The Bolt21 mobile app supports:

  • Screen reader compatibility (TalkBack on Android, VoiceOver on iOS)
  • Dynamic text sizing
  • High contrast mode
  • Biometric authentication as an alternative to PIN entry

Ongoing Efforts

We regularly review our website and app against WCAG 2.1 AA standards and research emerging best practices in accessibility. As an open source project, we welcome contributions that improve accessibility.

Legal Compliance

We aim to comply with applicable accessibility laws including:

  • Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)
  • Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act
  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Bolt21. If you encounter any barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • Email: support@bolt21.io
  • GitHub: Report an accessibility issue

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 2 business days.
